Campus Life

Our School Provides:

Spacious academic blocks surrounded with natural green environment.
Spacious, well - ventilated classrooms.
Airy, well-equiped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology & Computer Science.
Rich experience of qualified and trained faculties.
e-Book Library and well equiped Audio-Visual Room
Interactive learning and teaching environment through LCD projectors for both juniors and seniors.
A Teacher's Resource Cell providing the teachers every conceivable material to enhance self and enhance teaching.
Student's Resource Centre equipped with interactive educational CDs / DVDs
A Career Information Cell with a trained career counselor to keep senior school student abreast of latest developments on the career front.

Activity corners ( Clubs ) in class rooms for juniors and seniors.

RFID Biometrics Attendance System for both Teachers and Students.
